## SI Units

**length** -> m (meter) // ft (0.3048m)

  e.g. R (radius)

**mass** -> kg (kilogram) // slug (14.59 kg)

**time** -> s / sec (second)

### example

$$
\text{1 Newton}=1N = \dfrac{1kgm}{sec^2}
$$

## Dimension analysis

$$
\begin{align}
F & = ma \\
& = (2kg)(3m/sec^2) \\
& = 6\dfrac{kgm}{sec^2}
\end{align}
$$

$$
